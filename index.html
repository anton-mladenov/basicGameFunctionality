Welcome to Anton's Irish Inn Hero Game

Let's play a game.

Click on the hotel image and choose a name for your hero.

Then, you should choose a weapon.

Now, equip your hero with a weapon from your inventory.

If you have clicked on all weapons, enter an integer between 0 and 2 in the prompt box.

Finally, click on the enemy icon to engage in a fight.

The winner will be determined, based on your chosen weapon's damage and your enemy's strength.
